Perfect Leachate Treatment

SLC's stable leachate treatment is a leading global standard for water treatment technologies, and
the treated water is reused for process water, contributing to the protection of water resources.

What Is Leachate?

Leachate is wastewater that is generated by landfills. It has higher pollutant concentrations than typical sewage water.

Leading Global Leachate Treatment Technology

Leading leachate treatment technology We contribute to preventing marine pollution by succeeding in the integrated treatment of food waste effluent and leachate. In addition, we are playing a leading role in developing the best leachate treatment technology and providing the technology to landfill sites across the country.

What is Non-Discharge?

A non-discharge system prevents contamination of the surrounding environment by entirely recycling leachate, and food waste effluent, etc. coming from the landfill site for power plant cooling water, landscape gardening, and other general purposes.
